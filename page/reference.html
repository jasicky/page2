ASMPT DEK Horizon 03iX Solder Paste Printer




The ASMPT DEK Horizon 03iX is a high-performance SMT Screen Printer designed for precision, speed, and versatility. This Solder Paste Printer supports large substrates with a maximum print area of **510 × 508.5 mm** and substrate thickness from **0.2 mm to 6 mm**, making it ideal for varied PCB assemblies.  

Key features include:  
- Machine Alignment Capability: ±12.5 µm @ 2.0 Cmk (±6 Sigma) :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}  
- Process Alignment / Wet Printing Accuracy: ±25 µm (≥ 2.0 Cpk) :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  
- Core Cycle Time: about 8 sec + process (standard Horizon 03iX; option “HTC” may reduce to ~11-12s) :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  
- Transport & Handling: substrate sizes from small (≈50×40.5 mm) up to max, substrate weight up to ~1 kg, warpage tolerance up to ~7 mm including thickness; versatile transport directions (left-right, right-left, etc.):contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  
- Vision & Control: high-speed moving cameras (HawkEye 750 or optional 1700), dual LED lighting, semi-automatic screen frame width/mesh loading, DEK Instinctiv™ controller software, CAN-BUS motion control:contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}  
- Print Pressure & Pressure Mechanisms: 0-20 kg print pressure range, motorised scrapers, software-controlled feedback loops for squeegee pressure control:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}  

With the DEK Horizon 03iX, you get a reliable, large-format SMT Printer that balances throughput and accuracy, perfect for EMS lines, high-mix production, and applications requiring fine pitch printing. Whether you need a robust stencil printer for standard PCBs or a precision Solder Paste Printer for demanding requirements, the DEK Horizon 03iX delivers on quality and performance.
